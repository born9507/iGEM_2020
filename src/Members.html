KOREA-SIS

Korea-SIS is a high school iGEM team from Seoul International School. Having come together to provide a solution for the crop losses during storage in South Asian countries, our team consists of unique individuals who are all passionate about solving this problem. Through our combined efforts, our team was able to come up with a successful end result that shows the progress we made as a team.
